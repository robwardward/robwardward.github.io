Seam Editions was a creative-critical publishing project based variously in Norwich, London, and Reading.

The project was run by Alex Read, Sara Helen Binney, Sarah Jones, Sarah Taylor, Simon Pook, and Robert Ward.

Everything Seam published online, and detais of two print projects, is available here for posterity.

Thank you for reading.
